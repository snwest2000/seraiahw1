Projects

Project 1: "Video as a Cultural Critique"

This project focused on the concept of copyright and the usage of artists and creators using copywritten music.

Project 2: "Installation"

This project focused on the concept of projection. I wanted to show my passion for Marvel films and the characters by drawing them as each of them were killed by Thanos.

Project 3: "Critical Interactivity"

This project focused on the concept of netart and my first internet memory. I wanted to show how my somewhat conservative Christian parents saw YouTube and the alternative rock music I listened to as kid (and to this day).

Final Project: "Stan Lee / Jack Kirby Tribute"

The concept I am exploring in this video is go further and pushing my project from Project 2 (projection). I used Sofia Cordova's video as a reference to my project when adding video and photo layers on top of my video project. This video projection is supposed to be a tribute to Stan Lee (and Jack Kirby), who recently passed away on November 12th. I wanted to show how Stan Lee's legacy had an impact on my life and other fans.
